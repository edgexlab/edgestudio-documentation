# Plans

Your subscription plan determines the storage, CPU, and GPU limits available to you.

---

## Overview

The Plan card on the [Dashboard](/docs/dashboard) shows your current plan (Free, Pro, etc.) for whichever workspace you're in — Personal or an Organization. Personal and Organization plans are managed separately.

---

## Upgrading

Click **Upgrade** on the Plan card to move off the Free plan. Personal workspaces go through the standard subscription flow; Organization workspaces are upgraded from the organization's own upgrade page.

---

## Managing an Existing Plan

Once you're on a paid plan, click **Manage** to view billing details, change plans, or cancel.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **My plan shows "Loading..." indefinitely.**
  Refresh the page. If it persists, contact support.

- **I upgraded but my limits haven't updated.**
  Usage limits refresh shortly after a successful upgrade — if they still look wrong after a few minutes, contact support.
