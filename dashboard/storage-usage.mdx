# Storage Usage

How much storage your datasets, models, and builds are using against your plan's limit.

---

## Overview

The Storage card on the [Dashboard](/docs/dashboard) shows your used storage against your plan's total, with a progress bar. This includes uploaded datasets, trained models, and generated builds.

---

## Staying Under Your Limit

If you're approaching your storage limit:

- Delete datasets or projects you no longer need
- Remove old builds you don't plan to deploy
- See [Plans](/docs/dashboard/plans) to upgrade for more storage

---

## Troubleshooting

- **My storage usage seems too high.**
  Check for large or duplicate dataset uploads in unused projects.

- **I deleted files but usage hasn't dropped.**
  Storage numbers can take a few minutes to refresh after a deletion.
