# Dashboard

Your workspace home — plan, usage, and the projects you have access to.

---

## Overview

The Dashboard shows your current plan, resource usage (storage, CPU, GPU), and your list of projects. Everything on this page is scoped to whichever workspace you're currently in — your **Personal** workspace, or an **Organization** you're a member of.

---

## Current Plan

The Plan card shows your active plan (Free, Pro, etc.). Click **Upgrade** to move to a paid plan, or **Manage** to review/change your existing subscription.

---

## Usage (Storage, CPU, GPU)

Each usage card shows how much of your plan's storage, CPU minutes, and GPU minutes you've consumed, with a progress bar against your plan's limit. These reset according to your plan's billing cycle.

---

## Switching Workspaces

Use the workspace switcher (top right) to move between your **Personal** workspace and any **Organization** you belong to. Plan, usage, and the project list all update to reflect the workspace you select.

---

## Projects

The Projects table lists every project in the current workspace. From here you can:

- **Create** a new project
- **Open** a project to work on its devices, datasets, training, and deployment
- **Edit** a project's name/description
- **Delete** a project

See [Project](/docs/projects) for details on working inside a project once it's open.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **My plan/usage shows "Loading..." indefinitely.**
  Refresh the page. If it persists, your workspace's subscription data may have failed to load — contact support.

- **My usage numbers look wrong after switching workspaces.**
  Usage is workspace-specific — make sure you're checking the right one (Personal vs. Organization) in the switcher.
